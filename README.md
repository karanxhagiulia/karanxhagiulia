## Hej hej! I'm Giulia 👋

I'm a **Data & AI Change Enablement Specialist** based in Barcelona, working at the intersection of **analytics, AI adoption, internal communications, learning programmes, and product usage measurement**.

I help teams understand and adopt complex data and AI capabilities by turning technical ecosystems into clear narratives, practical guidance, scalable learning resources, and measurable enablement strategies.

Always learning 🌱

---

### 👩‍💻 What I work on

- Driving adoption of internal analytics and AI capabilities across global business teams  
- Creating enablement content, learning resources, newsletters, onboarding materials, guides, and documentation  
- Translating complex data products and technical concepts into clear communication for non-technical audiences  
- Supporting workshops, internal events, training sessions, and stakeholder engagement activities  
- Tracking engagement, learning uptake, certifications, product usage, and adoption signals  
- Exploring trustworthy AI, transparency, hallucination reduction, and responsible AI adoption
